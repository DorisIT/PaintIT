# User story 

Story Identifier: STKXXX
Story Name: Receive relevant words


## Description 

As a child, I need to receive words that I’m able to paint.

## Confirmation

* Let two persons play the game and notice if they are able to guess words correctly.
* Give a word and a paper to a random person and notice if they are able to paint the given word.

### Functional

   * Can I switch word if it’s too difficult to paint?
   * Can I choose between different words instead of a single one?

### Non-functional

Availability:
   * Can I play the game regardless my age (low word vocabulary)?


Security:
   * Can I edit the given word?
