# User story 

Story Identifier: STK001
Story Name: Hints


## Description 

As a player, I want to be able to get a hint towards the correct word in case my fellow player can’t draw (for shite);



## Confirmation


### Functional


* Can I reveal one or more letters in the correct word?
* Can I limit the letters I have to guess from?



### Non-functional

- availability
* Can I receive more than one hint (both during a round and a session)?
