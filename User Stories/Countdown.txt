# User story 


Story Identifier: STKXXX
Story Name: Countdown




## Description 


As a player, I need to know when my turn starts and have time to turn the computer around so that the other player can’t see the word that I am going to draw.


## Confirmation


### Functional


* Can I see how long time it is left before my turn starts?
* Do I have enough time to turn the computer around?
* Is it clear when my turn starts?


### Non-functional


Availability
* Can I start my turn at any time?


Security
* Can the other player start my turn immediately after his/her turn, so that she/he can see the word that I am going to draw?
